About Us

Spark Consultancy is an online platform for the advisory and consulting industry. The website presents the latest news and trends in the sector, follows the developments and publications of consulting firms across 60+ industries and functional areas and provides an overview of career opportunities for professionals interested in working in consultancy. We are part of the Consultancy.org family. Our international network of consulting platforms draws over 4 million visitors per year. Career opportunities span jobs in consulting, graduate schemes, apprenticeships and internships in the consulting industry, as well as job openings for professionals that want to work as a consultant outside the consulting industry. Spark Consultancy in addition presents company profiles of renowned consulting firms, provides an overview of events and offers key stats and sizes of the consulting industry. Spark Consultancy works together with top tier consulting firms active in India. Globally we work with more than 150 leading consulting firms and with a select number of partner companies. The newsletter of Spark Consultancy contains the most important news of the past week, interviews with consultants, jobs and events. Spark Consultancy has four social media channels: LinkedIn, Twitter, Facebook and Google+.
